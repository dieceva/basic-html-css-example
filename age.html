Age of Empires IV

Age of Empires is an old franchise that has evolved throughout time, along with their recurrent users. It is a middle aged themed game in which you play as a certain tribe or small society and you have to complete tasks in order to develop yours. Eventually you'll have a millitary to attack the enemies before they attack you in order to conquer the others, build an empire and win, hence the name of the game Age of Empires.
