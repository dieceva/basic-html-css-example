    Diego's favourite games

    Dota2

    Dota 2 is a strategy MOBA game in which each team (composing of 5 people) try to destroy the enemy's "ancient" which is kind of a relic inside each team's base and is guarded by 11 defensive towers in total.

    Every player can choose a Hero from a group of 120+ and this is called the draft. Drafting is one of the most important things in the game since many heroes counter others and you can combine their skills to make a good strategy.

    Out of the professional scenes of these games, Dota 2 is the one that is the biggest, with prize pools of millions of dollars every year.

    Clash Royale

    Clash Royale is a tower defense mobile game in which the objective is to destroy enemy towers and defend yours. It became very popular because it is the twin brother game of clash of clans. Gameplay is very different but characters of the game are almost the same, also both games were developed by the company Supercell.

    Age of Empires IV

    Age of Empires is an old franchise that has evolved throughout time, along with their recurrent users. It is a middle aged themed game in which you play as a certain tribe or small society and you have to complete tasks in order to develop yours. Eventually you'll have a millitary to attack the enemies before they attack you in order to conquer the others, build an empire and win, hence the name of the game Age of Empires.

    Fortnite Mods

    Fortnite is a very famous game that needs no introduction now. It is basically a battle royale with add-onns such as buildings, mods, etc. I don't really play this game too much, mostly with friends that have consoles since this game is cross-platform.
