    • Dota 2 is a strategy MOBA game in which each team (composing of 5 people) try to destroy the enemy's "ancient" which is kind of a relic inside each team's base and is guarded by 11 defensive towers in total.

    Every player can choose a Hero from a group of 120+ and this is called the draft. Drafting is one of the most important things in the game since many heroes counter others and you can combine their skills to make a good strategy.
